VIRTUE ETHICS

Virtue ethics is a theory that focuses on the character of the decision maker. Building a virtuous character requires practing the virtues until the moral agent knows the right thing to do in the right time in the right place in the right way. To begin, one must achieve a stable equilibrium of the soul by balancing various influences - both internal and external that might interfere with good judgement.
